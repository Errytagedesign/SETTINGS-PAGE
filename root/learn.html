What is a Dividend?

A dividend is a payment from a company to its shareholders, giving them a portion of the company’s earnings—Dividends are often paid quarterly and in cash.

What is a Portfolio?

A portfolio is a collection of financial assets, such as stocks, bonds, cash, real estate, or alternative investments

What is an ETF?

An exchange-traded fund (ETF) tracks multiple stocks or other securities to let you invest in a sector, industry, or even region—Through an ETF, you could also track an index, so you don’t have to pick individual stocks.
